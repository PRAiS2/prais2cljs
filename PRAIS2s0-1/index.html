This site is to help people make sense of the published survival statistics about children’s heart surgery.

Our website will help you to:

  • explore what survival rates can and can’t tell you
  • understand how the NHS monitors children’s heart surgery
  • explore published results for UK hospitals

Use the tabs or watch our animations to find out more!
