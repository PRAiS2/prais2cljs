UNDERSTANDING CHILDREN’S HEART SURGERY OUTCOMES

This site is to help people make sense of the published survival data about children’s heart surgery in the UK and Ireland.

Our website will help you:

  • explore what survival rates can and can’t tell you
  • understand how the NHS monitors children’s heart surgery
  • explore published data for hospitals in the UK and Ireland
